General Assembly

It is the policy making and representative organ of the UN.It is the only UN body with universal representation.Discussions on matter related to peace and security,admitting new members and budget are the major agendas in the General Assembly.However, these discussions require atleast a two-thirds majority of its members.

United Nations Security Council

It is accountable for making international peace and security.It also decides actions that should be taken by its member states to restore peace in zones of conflict.

Economic and Social Council

The Economic and Social Council serves as the central platform for discussing international economic,social and environmental issues and formulating policy recommendations.It plays a pivotal role in promoting sustainable development and coordinating efforts to address challenges that are faced globally.
